Voxel-Based

Voxel-based games are a type of video game that uses voxels, which are small, cube-shaped units, to create 3D graphics.

These games often have a distinctive blocky or pixelated aesthetic, and players can typically manipulate the game world by adding or removing voxels.

This allows for a high degree of flexibility and creativity in building and shaping the game world.

Voxel-based games have become increasingly popular in recent years, with games like Minecraft and Terraria attracting millions of players worldwide.
