Modding

The modding scene in Minecraft is a large, vibrant, and active community of players and developers creating and sharing mods that alter the gameplay and add new content to the game.

Mods can range from small changes to the game's mechanics to massive overhauls that add entirely new gameplay elements and features.

Players can install mods by using specialized software, such as Minecraft Forge, which allows them to easily manage and install mods.

Overall, the modding scene in Minecraft is a key part of the game's enduring popularity, as it allows players to customize and extend their gameplay experience in endless ways.
