Combat

In Minecraft, players can engage in combat with a variety of different mechanics.

Players can use weapons such as swords and bows to attack mobs or other players.

Players can also use shields to block incoming attacks.

Players can also use potions to gain temporary combat advantages, such as increased damage or resistance to damage.

In addition, players can use enchantments to imbue their weapons and armor with special abilities, such as increased damage or a chance to set enemies on fire.
